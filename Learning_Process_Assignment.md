# Learning Process

### Q.1 What is the Feynman Technique? Explain in 1 line?
Learn a concept by explaining it in simple language as if you're teaching it to a beginner, then identify and fill the gaps in your understanding.

### Q.3 What are active and diffused modes of thinking?
**Active (Focused) Mode**: When you concentrate deeply on a task and actively work to understand or solve a problem.

**Diffuse Mode**: When your mind is relaxed (walking, resting, showering, sleeping), allowing your brain to make connections and generate new ideas.


### Q.4 According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic? Only mention the points?


1. Break the topic into small chunks.
2. Focus on learning one chunk at a time.
3. Practice actively instead of just reading or watching.
4. Review and reinforce what you learned.
5. Connect the chunks to build a deeper understanding of the whole topic.
6. Schedule time consistently for learning and improvement.
