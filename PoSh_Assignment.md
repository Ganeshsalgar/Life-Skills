# Prevention of Sexual Harassment (PoSH)

## 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

- Unwanted touching or physical contact.
- Making sexual jokes or comments.
- Sending inappropriate messages or pictures.
- Repeatedly asking someone out after they say no.
- Any behaviour that makes a person feel unsafe or uncomfortable.

## 2. What would you do if you face or witness such behaviour?

- Stay calm and ensure everyone's safety.
- Report the incident to your manager, HR, or the Internal Committee (IC).
- Keep a record of what happened, if possible.
- Support the person affected.
- Do not ignore or encourage such behaviour.
